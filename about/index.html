ABOUT ME

While I consider myself a jack-of-all-trades, I specialize in creating professional, modern, and responsive websites. I have the most experience building sites "from scratch" including graphics and copy, but can easily integrate with existing code, content, and infrastructure.

I've worked with HTML/CSS, javascript, jQuery, frameworks like Bootstrap, WordPress and similar CMS platforms, SEO tools, and Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Brackets. In my free time, I'm exploring back-end languages like PHP and Python. I'm also familiar with Windows, Mac OS, and Linux, so I'm comfortable with the command line.

When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me practicing yoga, trying out new vegan recipes, cycling through Spotify playlists, or playing video games. I currently work as a web developer in the Chicago area. I am available for freelance work.
